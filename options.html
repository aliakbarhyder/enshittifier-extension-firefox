💩 Enshittifier

Your Dead Internet companion. Replace all mentions of AI with .

Type something in Custom — even a single character works.

Subdomains included automatically. * is a wildcard — e.g. *.openai.com, news.*, *reddit*.

Examples

  • Tell me about AI → Tell me about
  • ClosedAI's new model → Closed's new model
  • AI-powered toaster → -powered toaster
